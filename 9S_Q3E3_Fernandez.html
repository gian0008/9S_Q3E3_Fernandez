Tiger Woods
Tiger Woods is a professional golfer considered as one of the greatest golfers of all time. Woods is also one of the most famous and highest-paid athletes who holds numerous records in golf. 15-time major winner, Woods has earned over a billion dollars from endorsements, appearances, course design fees, etc and is among the richest athlete in the world. $800 million
